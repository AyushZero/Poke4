How to Play

Objective

Connect 4 pieces of your color in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to win!

Basic Rules

  • Players alternate turns dropping colored pieces into columns
  • Pieces fall to the lowest available position in the column
  • First player to get 4 in a row wins!

Pokémon Type Mechanics

This version adds Pokémon type interactions:

  • Each piece has a random Pokémon type
  • Your next type is shown in your sidebar
  • When a piece lands directly on an opponent's piece, they interact based on type effectiveness

Type Interactions

  • Super Effective (2×): Your piece destroys the opponent's piece below and falls into its space
  • Not Very Effective (0.5×): Opponent's piece destroys your incoming piece (your piece doesn't get placed)
  • No Effect (0×): Immunity - destroys the opponent's piece
  • No Team Kills: Your pieces never interact with your own pieces

Features

  • Type Chart: View all type matchups
  • Assist Mode: Toggle to see your current type's strengths and weaknesses
  • Logs: Review all type interactions during the game
